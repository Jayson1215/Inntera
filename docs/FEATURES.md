# Platform Features Guide

Inntera is divided into three distinct functional areas, each optimized for its specific user base.

## 👑 Admin Partner Hub
The Admin Hub is the mission control for property owners and managers.

- **Global Dashboard**: Visual analytics for bookings, revenue, and occupancy.
- **Hotel Management**: Create and manage multiple hotel properties with custom addresses and imagery.
- **Room Type Architect**: Define room categories (e.g., Suite, Deluxe) and set base prices.
- **Inventory Control**: Bulk management of individual rooms across all properties.
- **Dynamic Rates**: Update room rates in real-time based on seasonality or demand.
- **Personnel Hub**: Staff management, including account suspension and role assignments.
- **Guest Ledger**: Comprehensive database of all registered guests with security controls (Ban/Unban).

## 🛠️ Staff Operations Terminal
Designed for high-efficiency front-desk and housekeeping operations.

- **Arrivals & Departures**: Quick search and processing for guest check-ins and check-outs.
- **Automated Settlement**: Automatic recording of remaining balances upon check-in to ensure 100% collection.
- **Real-time Status Bar**: Quick-action buttons for room turnover (Ready, Occupied, Housekeeping, Maintenance).
- **Guest Intel**: View guest notes, special requests, and payment history during check-in.
- **Notification Center**: Instant alerts for new bookings or internal updates.

## 🏨 Client Guest Portal
A premium booking experience focused on ease of use and transparency.

- **Destination Discovery**: Search properties by city with real-time price calculation.
- **Smart Filters**: Filter rooms by occupancy requirements and property amenities.
- **Transparent Settlement**: Clear breakdown of downpayments, remaining balances, and payment methods.
- **Self-Service Vault**:
    - Manage upcoming stays.
    - Submit E-wallet (GCash/Maya) payment references.
    - Download PDF receipts for completed stays.
    - Secure cancellation policy enforcement (Restricted for in-residence guests).
