# 🚀 Inntera: Full Functionality Matrix

Inntera is an enterprise hospitality OS designed to handle every aspect of hotel management through specialized, role-optimized terminals.

---

## 👑 01. Admin Partner Hub
*The central nervous system for property owners and corporate managers.*

### 📊 Financial & Strategic Intelligence
- **Global Dashboard**: Real-time visualization of total revenue, occupancy rates, and active reservations.
- **Revenue Analytics**: Filtered views to track performance across different hotels or time periods.
- **Dynamic Rate Engine**: Instant update of room prices (`Base Price`) that propagates globally to the Guest Portal.

### 🏨 Property & Asset Orchestration
- **Property Management**: Full lifecycle management of hotel locations, including address data, contact details, and premium imagery.
- **Room Type Architecture**: Define and categorize room classes (e.g., *Penthouse*, *Garden Suite*) with specific capacity and pricing rules.
- **Inventory Matrix**: Bulk creation and management of individual room units with floor assignments.

### 👥 Identity & Security Control
- **Staff Provisioning**: Create and manage staff accounts, with the ability to **Suspend** or **Reactivate** access instantly.
- **Guest Registry**: A master database of all platform guests with a **Security Ban** feature to prevent problematic users from re-booking.
- **Platform Oversight**: Direct control over any reservation in the system, including force-cancellation and deletion.

---

## ⚡ 02. Staff Operations Terminal
*The high-velocity interface for front-desk and housekeeping teams.*

### 🛎️ Guest Transition Management
- **Arrivals Dashboard**: A prioritized view of "Confirmed" bookings ready for check-in.
- **Smart Check-in Flow**:
    - Automatic balance calculation based on `payments` history.
    - Settlement recording for remaining balances.
    - Physical room assignment from inventory.
- **Departure Processing**: Transitioning bookings to `checked-out` status, triggering housekeeping turnover.

### 🧹 Inventory Turnover Matrix
- **Real-time Status Control**: Dedicated controls to transition rooms between `available`, `occupied`, `housekeeping`, and `maintenance`.
- **Occupancy Tracking**: High-contrast badges and tooltips to monitor guest residence.

### 📝 Operational Efficiency
- **Reference ID Verification**: A dedicated process for staff to review PayMongo reference IDs submitted by guests, ensuring financial security.
- **Command Notifications**: Real-time alerts for new reservations or internal management updates.

---

## ✨ 03. Client Guest Portal
*A frictionless, premium digital experience for travelers.*

### 🔍 Discovery & Search
- **Property Exploration**: Destination-based hotel discovery with high-fidelity imagery and detailed room-type descriptions.
- **Instant Quotes**: Real-time calculation of total stay costs including taxes and fees.

### 💳 Integrated Settlement Vault
- **PayMongo Integration**: Native support for GCash, Maya, and GrabPay.
- **Self-Service Verification**: Guests input their transaction reference ID directly into the portal to transition bookings from `pending` to `confirmed`.

### 🛡️ Reservation Management
- **Digital Ledger**: Access to upcoming and historical reservations.
- **Dynamic Receipts**: Instant PDF generation for confirmed bookings.
